Basic actions
--------------
Move
Jump
"Action"
Inventory on number keys 1-5

Hats
--------------
Camo (default)
	Invisibility & can't move.
	ONLY works if velocity is zero.
Bunny Hat
	Run fast and jump high
	Limited time
Spanish Horn
	Shoot a distraction noise.
	Enemies immediately pursue the noise,
	even if they are pursuing the player.
	5-second time limit
	Only one at a time.
	Cooldown: 12 seconds
Pipe
	Throw a smoke bomb
	10-second time limit
	Also, only one exists at a time.
	Without impact, detonates after 5 tiles
	This MIGHT need a cooldown to be not broken.
Spring Hat
	Turn upside down and BOUNCE!
